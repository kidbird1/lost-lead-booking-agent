# Knowledge

This folder stores long-form research and source-backed notes.

Use it for:

- provider research
- competitor research
- buyer research
- pricing research
- compliance notes
- extracted PDF notes
- sales examples

Do not store:

- API keys
- real customer phone numbers
- private call transcripts
- payment data

## Structure

```text
knowledge/research/
knowledge/provider-notes/
knowledge/sales/
knowledge/compliance/
```

## Rule

Research should become decisions, docs, or backlog items.

Do not let research become a pile of unused notes.
