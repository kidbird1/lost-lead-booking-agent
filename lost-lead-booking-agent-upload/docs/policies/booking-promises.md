# Booking Promises Policy

## Rule

The agent saves appointment requests by default.

## Required Language

Use:

```text
I saved your appointment request. The team will confirm.
```

## Do Not Say

- Do not promise a confirmed calendar booking unless live calendar booking is enabled and the backend confirms the event.
- Do not quote exact prices.
- Do not promise emergency arrival.
- Do not promise service outside the configured service area.

## Human Review

Send unclear, outside-area, emergency, or unavailable-time requests to owner follow-up.

## Success Metric

The caller understands the request is saved, and the owner has enough information to confirm or reject it.
