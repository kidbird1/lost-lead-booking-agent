# WhatsApp Plan

## Why Use WhatsApp

You already have Twilio.

WhatsApp can be useful because many people trust it and respond faster than email.

## What WhatsApp Should Do

- confirm appointments
- send reminders
- notify the owner
- follow up with old leads
- send "we got your request" messages

## What WhatsApp Should Not Do First

- replace phone calls
- handle emergencies
- send bulk marketing without consent
- become a full chatbot before booking works

## First Use

After the AI books a call:

Customer message:

```text
Your appointment with [Business Name] is booked for [Date/Time]. Reply here if you need to update anything.
```

Owner message:

```text
New booked job: [Name], [Service], [Address], [Urgency], [Date/Time].
```

## Fallback

If WhatsApp fails, use SMS.
