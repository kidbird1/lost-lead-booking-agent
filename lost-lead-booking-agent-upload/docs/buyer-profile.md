# Buyer Profile

## Best First Buyer

A small home service owner who already gets calls.

They should have:

- existing customer demand
- missed calls
- jobs worth at least a few hundred dollars
- a simple appointment process
- willingness to pay monthly

## Bad First Buyer

Avoid:

- brand-new businesses with no calls
- very tiny budgets
- businesses with complex dispatch rules
- regulated emergency workflows
- owners who want full custom software before testing

## Buying Trigger

They recently lost a job because they missed a call.

They are spending money on ads.

They cannot afford a full receptionist.

They hate answering calls during work.

## Simple Sales Line

You already paid to make the phone ring. We make sure someone answers.
