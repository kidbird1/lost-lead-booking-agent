# Sales Playbook

## Offer

You already paid to make the phone ring. We make sure someone answers.

## Simple Pitch

```text
We install an AI booking assistant for home service businesses.

When you miss a call, it answers, collects the job details, books the appointment or sends you a summary, and follows up by text.

Goal: fewer missed jobs, more booked estimates, less phone stress.
```

## Best First Buyer

Roofing or HVAC owner who:

- gets inbound calls
- works in the field
- misses calls
- spends money on ads
- has jobs worth hundreds or thousands

## First Outreach

```text
Hey [Name], quick question.

When someone calls after hours or while your team is on a job, do those calls usually get answered?

I'm setting up an AI booking assistant for [roofers/HVAC companies] that answers missed calls, collects job details, and books estimates.

If it does not capture at least one missed opportunity in the first 30 days, you should not keep it.
```

## Demo Script

```text
Call the demo number.
Pretend to be a homeowner with a leak.
AI answers.
AI collects details.
AI books or flags follow-up.
Owner gets summary.
```

## Pilot Offer

```text
30-day missed-call recovery pilot.

Setup: low or free for first test client.
Monthly after pilot: $300-$800/month depending on call volume.
```

## Buyer Questions

```text
How many calls do you get per week?
How many do you miss?
Do you run ads?
What is an average booked job worth?
Who answers calls now?
What happens after hours?
Do you use Google Calendar, Jobber, Housecall Pro, or ServiceTitan?
Do you want AI to book directly or just collect details first?
```
