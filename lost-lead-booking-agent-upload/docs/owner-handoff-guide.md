# Owner Handoff Guide

Use this after a client passes the live test call.

## What The Agent Does

```text
Missed call -> AI answers -> collects job details -> saves lead -> alerts owner
```

The agent saves appointment requests. The team confirms the final schedule.

## What The Owner Receives

- WhatsApp or SMS alert with caller details.
- Private lead viewer link.
- Lead status buttons:
  - Follow up
  - Contacted
  - Booked
  - Lost

## Daily Owner Routine

1. Open the lead viewer.
2. Call or text new leads.
3. Mark each lead as contacted, booked, lost, or follow-up.
4. Tell the operator if an alert or call looks wrong.

## Owner Rules

- The AI does not quote prices.
- The AI does not promise emergency response.
- The AI does not guarantee calendar booking unless calendar booking is enabled.
- If a request is unclear, the lead is marked for follow-up.
