# Product Brief

## Name

Lost Lead Booking Agent

## Buyer

Home service business owners.

Examples:

- roofers
- HVAC companies
- plumbers
- electricians
- landscapers
- remodelers
- cleaners

## Pain

They miss calls while working, driving, sleeping, or after hours.

Missed call means lost job.

## Why It Costs Money

Customers do not wait.

If nobody answers, they call the next company.

## Product

A 24/7 AI phone agent that answers missed calls, asks what the customer needs, books the appointment, texts the customer, and sends the owner a summary.

## First MVP

One business.

One phone number.

One AI call flow.

One Google Calendar.

One owner notification text.

One lead log.

## Success Metric

Booked jobs from calls that would have been missed.
